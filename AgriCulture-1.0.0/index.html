Transforming Agriculture Across West Africa

At Cape Investment Sierra Ltd., we are transforming agriculture in Sierra Leone and across West Africa through large-scale smart farming, sustainable livestock management, agro-processing and global agricultural trade.

Over 5,000 Hectares of Fertile Agricultural Land

Located at Smart Farm, Freetown with major agricultural operations in Lunsar, our integrated agricultural project spans over 5,000 hectares of fertile land, with future expansion potential of over 12,500 acres and up to 50,000 acres within the region.

Modern Technology, Fully Integrated Ecosystem

Our operations combine modern farming technology, agricultural machinery, greenhouse cultivation, irrigation systems and agro-processing infrastructure to create a fully integrated agricultural ecosystem.

From Rice Fields to Dairy, Poultry & Goat Farming

From premium rice cultivation and organic vegetable farming to large-scale dairy, poultry and goat farming, our mission is to produce high-quality agricultural products while contributing to food security, economic development and global agricultural supply chains.

Supplying Organic Products to Europe & Asia

We proudly supply organic agricultural products to European markets, meet the growing cashew demand in Asian countries, export premium teak wood and facilitate international agricultural trade including sugar imports from Brazil.

    Rice Milling & Agro Processing

    Our project includes a modern rice milling facility equipped with:

    • Rice milling plant with 2.5 tons per hour processing capacity
    • Paddy cleaning and de-husking systems
    • Rice polishing and whitening units
    • Packing and bagging facility
    • Grain warehouses and storage silos

    The rice mill enables us to process paddy into high-quality edible rice for local markets and international supply chains.

    Global Agricultural Trade

    With extensive agricultural expertise and resources, we actively participate in international trade of agricultural commodities.

    Our global trade activities include:

    • Export of organic agricultural products to Europe
    • High-demand cashew exports to Asian markets
    • Export of premium teak wood

    Our proximity to Freetown international seaport and airport enables efficient global logistics.
