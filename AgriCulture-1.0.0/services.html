Commercial Crop Farming

We specialize in large-scale crop production using modern farming techniques.

Our farming operations include:

02
Rice Milling & Processing

Our modern rice mill processes paddy through:

  • Pre-cleaning and paddy separation
  • De-husking and bran removal
  • Rice polishing and whitening
  • Packing and bagging

This ensures high-quality rice production for local markets and international trade.
